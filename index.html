Roof Refresher

Heat-Responsive Roof Cooling System with Water Conservation Integration

View 3D Design Explore System

Project Summary

The Roof Refresher aims to reduce indoor heat in residential homes by using a sensor-activated sprinkler system. Designed for tropical regions like the Philippines, it offers a sustainable, low-cost solution to help households manage extreme temperatures.

Materials Used

LM35 Temperature Sensor

Monitors roof temperature and activates the system at 35°C.

Mini Water Pump

Sprays water onto the roof surface during high temperatures.

Relay Module

Controls the electrical switch to the pump and sprinklers.

16x2 LCD Display

Displays real-time temperature, countdown, and system status.

Arduino Uno

Main microcontroller that runs the automated cooling program.

Mini Lightbulb

Indicates when the system is active or inactive.

Push Button

Manually starts or stops the system or resets the program.

Resistor

Protects components from excess electrical current.

Jumper Wires

Connects components across the breadboard and Arduino.

Breadboard

Allows prototyping without soldering for easy connections.

System Overview

The Roof Refresher system automatically activates when the LM35 detects temperatures at or above 35°C. A relay triggers the water pump, which sprays water for 10 seconds, then shuts off to conserve water. The LCD displays system status and countdown.
